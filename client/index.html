PicSearch - an image searching tool created with full stack Javascript.



Search for images by passing keywords in the URL, and use ?offset to skip through results.

Visit /api/latest/imagesearch to see the most recent search terms.


Example Usage:

http://picsearch.herokuapp.com/api/imagesearch/Game of Thrones
http://picsearch.herokuapp.com/api/imagesearch/Game of Thrones?offset=10
http://picsearch.herokuapp.com/api/imagesearch/cybergoths
http://picsearch.herokuapp.com/api/imagesearch/Jumanji?offset=25
http://picsearch.herokuapp.com/api/latest/imagesearch
